Ordering and shipping information*

*Please note that this website was made for educational purposes and all the information written below are invented

Order preparation
From the moment you place an order on Bobooky, the time required to prepare your package and then ship it depends on the availability of the products. This parameter varies from product to product, so if you make a multiple order consider that the shipment will be complete after all the products ordered will be available.


Shipment
Once the package has been prepared with all the products you have purchased, we will ship it to you with the shipping method you indicated. Before concluding the purchase we will offer you an estimated shipping date. If, during order fulfillment, unforeseen events (delays, exhaustion of a product, etc.) occur, we will promptly inform you by e-mail of the status of the order.


Delivery
Once the package is ready, the delivery time of your order depends on the shipping method you have chosen. On the order summary page, after choosing the delivery method, we will already inform you of the estimated date on which your purchases will be delivered to your home.
